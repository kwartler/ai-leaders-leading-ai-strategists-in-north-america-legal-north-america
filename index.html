What conferences should I attend for Legal AI?

Key events include Legaltech, ILTACON, the Legal AI Forum, and ABA TECHSHOW. These conferences bring together legal professionals, technologists, and AI researchers focused on transforming legal practice.

How can I grow as an AI leader within Legal?

Legal AI leaders grow by contributing to bar association AI ethics guidelines, publishing on AI's impact on legal practice, and building bridges between law firms and legal technology startups. Pro bono work deploying AI for access to justice initiatives demonstrates values-driven leadership.
